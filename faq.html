FAQ

Quick answers to common towing questions.

What information should I have when I call?
Location (or nearest intersection), vehicle type, what happened, and your destination (home/shop).
Do you tow 24/7?
Yes — dispatch is available 24/7. ETAs vary with traffic, weather, and call volume.
Can you help with lockouts or dead batteries?
Yes — roadside calls include jump starts and lockouts. Call for availability and pricing.
Do you offer long distance towing?
Often yes, depending on schedule and vehicle type. Call dispatch to confirm.
Can you recover a vehicle off-road or in a ditch?
Possibly — recovery depends on access, safety, and conditions. We’ll assess and explain options.
