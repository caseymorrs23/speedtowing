FAQ

Quick answers to common towing and roadside questions.

Call Dispatch

How fast can you arrive?

Response time depends on traffic, call volume, and your exact location. Calling with clear cross‑streets speeds dispatch.

Do you do flatbeds?

Flatbed availability can vary by unit and job. If you need flatbed (low clearance, AWD, etc.), tell dispatch.

What info do you need?

Location, destination, vehicle make/model, whether it rolls/steers, and any access issues (locked wheels, key lost).

Can you tow to my shop?

Yes—give dispatch the shop name and address. If the shop is closed, we can coordinate a safe drop‑off if allowed.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing typically depends on distance, time, vehicle type, and complexity (winch‑out, access issues, etc.). Call for a quote.

Is it better to call or use the form?

For urgent roadside situations: call. The online request is best for scheduled or non‑urgent tows.
